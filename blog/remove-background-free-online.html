How to Remove Image Background Free — Without Uploading Your Photo

Remove.bg is the most popular background removal tool. It works well. It also uploads every photo you process to its servers — where it's processed by their AI, temporarily stored and then deleted.

For product photos and marketing images that's fine. For personal photos, ID documents, portraits or anything with faces — you might not want a copy of your image on a third-party server.

How to remove a background — step by step

  1. Go to rightimagekit.com and click Remove Background
  2. Upload your image — JPG or PNG with a clear subject
  3. The AI model loads locally in your browser (first time: ~15MB download)
  4. Click Remove Background
  5. Download the PNG with transparent background

What makes a good background removal image?

  • Clear subject contrast — person or object that stands out from the background
  • Good lighting — even light, no harsh shadows across the subject edge
  • Uncluttered background — solid colours or simple scenes work best
  • Sharp edges — hair and fine detail is harder but works well with modern AI

Why is it a Pro feature?

Background removal requires loading a large AI model (~15MB WASM file) into your browser. That's the cost of running it locally instead of on a server. The Pro plan (£4.99/month) covers the Claude API costs for AI tools and the infrastructure for distributing the WASM model efficiently.
