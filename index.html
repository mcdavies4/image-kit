Questions

Are my images uploaded anywhere?

No. Every free tool runs entirely in your browser. Your image data is never sent to a server, which is the whole point of RightImageKit.

Do I need to sign up?

Never. There's no account, no email, no cookie wall for your files. Open the page and start working.

What's in the £4.99 Pro version?

A one-time unlock for the AI-assisted tools — background removal, upscaling, photo enhancement and automatic alt-text. The free tools stay free forever.

Why does removing metadata matter?

Photos can carry hidden EXIF data including the exact GPS coordinates where they were taken and your device model. Re-exporting through RightImageKit removes it.

Which formats are supported?

PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF and BMP for input. You can export to PNG, JPEG and WebP.
